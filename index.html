Introduction

What is Animal Husbandry?

Animal Husbandry is the branch of agriculture concerned with the care, breeding, and raising of livestock such as cows, buffaloes, goats, poultry, and fish.

Importance in India:

India has a large rural population depending on animals for income, dairy, food, and farming needs. Animal husbandry plays a key role in supporting agriculture, nutrition, and the rural economy.
